Being that I work from home, I tend to spend a lot of my time at coffee shops these days. Little Amps Coffee Roasters is located in downtown Harrisburg and I have to say it's one of the best places in the city to get some great coffee. Everyone who works there are passionate and take time to craft each and every cup or shot you order. As well as having quality coffee, it also has a clean, hipster vibe.
