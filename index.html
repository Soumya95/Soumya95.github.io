WHO AM I?

I have always wanted to answer that question belting out “I'm Jean Valjean” but, despite my enthusiasm, that is not my name nor is bursting into song one of my talents.

My name is Lucia and what I really am good at is making lovely things. I have been doing it in different ways and places for almost a decade. First at a tiny design studio in my hometown, later at an in-house position when I moved to Madrid, then at a cool agency where I got to work for big clients, and after that flying solo as an independent designer.

Now I have made my dream come true, I have moved to London and I am looking for my next adventure.

I know, the question remains unanswered. Who am I? Well, I am a female of the human species, lover of unicorns and flamingos, cake eater, fan of overdressing and occasional damsel in distress. And right now I am also a person looking for a job.

Oh, and I am afraid of pigeons.
