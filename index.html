Hello.

I'm a digital designer and photographer. For more than 3 years, I have been building sites & designing experience in the browser. I'm currently working at Cabbage Tree Creative.

I'm a Christian. Born in the Philippines in a small town called Villasis. After gaining my degree, I moved to the charming country of New Zealand together with my family and Tina, my lovely wife. My passion for design can be traced back to my hobby of doodling stuff on school arm chairs from an early age.
